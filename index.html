Janasnehi - Karnataka

Bridging Public and Governance

Receive and respond to any query, grievance, feedback from the public on any issues related to government and governance across related departments.

Roles and Functions

Karnataka Department of Information and Public Relations initiative.
JanaSnehi acts as a bridge between Citizens and State Government. 

Always available

Available 24x7 for 365 days of the year. To provide constant support and assistance to Public on civic matters.

Fast

Respond within 30 minutes with a docket number or provide information sought after. If the issue is actionable, help the citizen in resolving it by coordinating with the concerned stakeholder.

Mobile-first

Available to provide support via Twitter, Email, Facebook, Whatsapp and Telegram
